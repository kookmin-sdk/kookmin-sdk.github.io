# Welcome to SCRC

**Special Communications & Convergence Service Research Center**

---

## Organization

Our center focuses on two important areas of study:

1. **System Development** - Developing advanced communication systems and solutions
2. **Standardization** - Establishing standards based on our research results

---

## System Development

Our center conducts cutting-edge research and development in communication system technologies across various challenging environments.

**[More on System Development](/laboratory/)**

---

## Standardization

We work on standardizing research results to create industry-standard solutions and protocols.

**[More on Standardization](/projects/)**

---

## Research Projects

The center conducts numerous national R&D projects every year in various fields such as:
- Underwater communication networks
- Maritime communication systems
- Polar region communication networks
- Special communication technologies
